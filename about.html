I'm a French designer living in the Alps with over a decade of experience crafting interfaces. I stumbled upon design by accident during my studies (I have a Master in Corporate Finance 😨) while creating a website for my partner's business. Since then I’ve become a specialist in product design, interactions and visual design. I’m currently Creative Director at Metalab and have been refining my craft there for 7 years now.

I’ve been lucky enough to work for many different clients and industries, always animated by the same goal, creating unique, beautiful, memorable and highly usable interfaces that people love to use.  

Additionally, I co-founded Deltaboard, a company that designs and manufactures products for climbing enthusiasts who want to train at home.  Our initial product, the first fully modular climbing hangboard, was successfully funded through a Kickstarter campaign in 2020. We've since launched a modular, self-supporting stand for any hangboard and continued improving our original hangboard.
