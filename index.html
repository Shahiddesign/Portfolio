Sorare

Sorare is a fantasy sport and NFT collectible game. I designed their iOS app from scratch and collaborated with our team to create NBA and Baseball NFT cards that are at the heart of the game.

I led the entire creative direction, revamped their web experience and supported the efforts in redefining their brand identity. I played a key role in expanding their platform to include new sports (NBA and MBL) . With the team, we delivered an exciting and rewarding gaming experience for a rapidly growing community of sports fans and users.

Pitch

Pitch mission is to make the best product for presentation, focusing on collaboration, ease of use and beautification.

Alongside the Pitch team, I conceptualized the core user experience and visual layer of the product. Our approach was to create a de-cluttered user interface that gradually unveils features and options as needed. The result is an intuitive and visually appealing platform that people enjoy using daily to present their best work and ideas. This unique approach sets us apart from our competitors and has helped establish Pitch as the go-to solution for modern presentations.

The Athletic

The Athletic is a leading subscription-based sports news platform that aimed to expand its audience by introducing new features.

I also explored ways to keep fans informed about their favorite sports events, from the Olympics to world tournaments. These efforts helped The Athletic expand its audience and deliver unparalleled value to sports enthusiasts worldwide.

I'm a UI/UX Designer designer living in the Lahore with over 5 Years of experience crafting interfaces. I stumbled upon design by accident during my studies while creating a website for my partner's business. Since then I’ve become a specialist in product design, interactions and visual design. I’m currently Creative Designer at Metalab and have been refining my craft there for 2 years now.

I’ve been lucky enough to work for many different clients and industries, always animated by the same goal, creating unique, beautiful, memorable and highly usable interfaces that people love to use.  

Additionally, I have also Worked with Deltaboard, a company that designs and manufactures products for climbing enthusiasts who want to train at home.  Our initial product, the first fully modular climbing hangboard, was successfully funded through a Kickstarter campaign in 2020. We've since launched a modular, self-supporting stand for any hangboard and continued improving our original hangboard.
